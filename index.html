Our History

The word “ Yäwäl ” refers to concepts like communal,inclusive,and shared.Coffee in Ethiopia is “ Yäwäl ” by everyone,it is harvested by the community ,produced collectively,brings the community together to celebrate and wish the best to one another.However Coffee was first found in Ethiopia,it unites the whole world in its unique taste. “Yäwäl” Coffee is 100% Ethiopian Arabic Coffee, but kept the standard value of world coffee,it serves everyone inclusively, preserve people's shared cultural integrity. “Yäwäl” Coffee is hand-picked from Ethiopia and prepared carefully to protect its genuine taste of nature. “ Yäwäl ” Coffee pursue to quench the thirst of true coffee lovers by true coffee
